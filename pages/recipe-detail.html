  • Chicken (cut into pieces) 500g
  • Basmati Rice 300g
  • Yogurt 200ml
  • Onion (sliced) 3 medium
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste 2 tbsp
  • Biryani Masala 2 tsp
  • Ghee 100ml
  • Salt to taste As per taste

ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ Cooking Instructions

1 Marinate the Chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

2 Fry the Onions

Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pot and fry sliced onions until golden brown. Remove and set aside.

3 Cook the Rice

Boil rice in salted water until 70% cooked. Drain well before using.

4 Layer and Cook

In the pot with ghee, place marinated chicken, then a layer of rice, then fried onions. Repeat layers. Cover and cook on high heat for 2 minutes, then low heat for 30-40 minutes.

5 Serve

Let it rest for 5 minutes. Gently mix and serve hot with raita and salad.
